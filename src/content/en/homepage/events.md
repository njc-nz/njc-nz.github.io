*Beginner, Peewee, and Junior classes are run during school terms and not on public holidays.
Senior and Jurassic Judo classes generally continue through school holidays (between terms) and on public holidays.*


#### Van tournaments
These are friendly competitions between the clubs of the Central Districts Area - Nelson Judo Club, Motueka Judo Club, Blenheim Judo Club, and Granity Judo Club. The clubs take turns hosting. While primarily for the kids, there are often opportunities for adults to compete too. Van tournaments are great opportunities to get to know the other judoka and their families in our region. 

#### Competitions
Judo New Zealand holds several competitions every year. These draw judoka from across the country as well as from overseas. Divisions are based on gender, age, and weight. 
See the Judo New Zealand Events Calendar for up to date information: Judo New Zealand
