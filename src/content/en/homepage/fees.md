We operate using the following fees:

![Fees](images/fees.png)


## Family discount

A $10 per member discount will be applied to a family's total term fees when there are two or more club members from the same family. E.g. Term fees for a family with 2 children: (2 x $85) - (2 x $10) = $150 per term.

## Judo New Zealand Fees

Judo New Zealand Annual Registration: $110/annum.

Grading: $50/grading.

Competition fees - for those who enter competitions. This will be circulated at the time.
