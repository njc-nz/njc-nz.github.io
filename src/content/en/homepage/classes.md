#### New to Judo? Give it a go!


![Judo Fam](/images/about-us1.png)


We welcome everyone to come try judo! Whether you’ve just heard about judo or you’ve dabbled in some similar sports or martial arts, you’re welcome to come along to a class to see if you like it. 

We offer a couple of trial classes so that you can decide if you want to continue. Everyone who gets on the mat must have their details registered with our club in case of an emergency. This can be done at: http://nelsonjudo.clubstuff.nz

• <strong>Beginners</strong> - open to beginners of all ages. A great place to start judo, learn some fundamentals before progressing into a juniors or seniors class.

• <strong>Juniors</strong> - mainly 7- 13 year olds. All experience levels are welcome.

• <strong>Seniors</strong> - teenagers and adults. All experience levels are welcome. 
</br>
• <strong>Women's class</strong> - a class for women, run by women, all grades welcome.
</br>
• <strong>Jurrasic-judo</strong> - fundamentals/technique-focused class for all grades and ages (mostly attended by adults)
